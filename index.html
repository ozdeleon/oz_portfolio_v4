Yahoo Finance

Email Project

Created all assets and looks for engagement and retention emails. Delivered multiple looks for A/B testing. Pushed for a younger, fun and current style for the art direction focusing on patterns, bright colors and animated GIFs.

Huffpost

Advertising

Created new looks for mobile ads for Huffpost’s push for mobile user acquisition. Drawing inspiration from their three major strengths: Politics, Entertainment, and Lifestyle News. Found a striking balance between imagery and minimalist color.

Seamus Turner

web

Minimalist website for Seamus Turner, Folk musician from California. Website features a two-color palette, an Angular music player that leverages the Soundcloud API, and a parallax effect that can be interacted with via mouse on desktop or accelerometer on mobile. Used combination of HTML, CSS, and multiple Javascript libraries. Also used Photoshop and Illustrator to create all assets. Visit site.

Breakout Breakfast

Media

Breakout Breakfast is a collaboration from Yahoo Finance and Build Studio. I was tasked with updating their mobile banners, emails, social posts and video slates for live broadcast.

Makers

Social Posts

MAKERS is a storytelling platform for the trailblazing women of today and tomorrow. I helped develop social post templates divided into multiple themes that spanned the course of a month. I found the use of the brand’s primary colors were an opportunity to push the style forward. My main focus was to highlight not only aesthetics, but the women and their powerful messages.
